About Us

Non-Profit Organization

We are a group of incoming high school juniors committed to addressing food insecurity and hunger. While we aspire to expand our impact in the future, our current focus is on serving the Katy-Houston area. Each month, we organize a unique and engaging event designed to raise donations for our cause and raise awareness within our community. For every event, we select a specific shelter to receive the proceeds. We invite you to join us in our mission to combat hunger and make a meaningful difference.

Our Opportunities

Community Meal Prep & Distribution

Volunteers can help prepare, package, and distribute fresh, nutritious meals to individuals and families facing food insecurity. Whether you're chopping vegetables in the kitchen, assembling meal kits, or delivering food directly to people’s doors, your efforts ensure that no one in our community is left hungry. This hands-on opportunity is perfect for anyone who wants to make an immediate, tangible difference.

Monthly Events

At Seeds of Hope, we host monthly events in order to provide the communities with opportunities to gain awareness regarding our project, as well as ways to take action and help us. If you are creative and have ideas on how to create engaging events that would help gain popularity, you can join us by helping us organize these events.

Education & Outreach

Sometimes, the most powerful tool we can offer is knowledge. Volunteers in our outreach team help spread awareness about Seeds of Hope's programs, connect families with food resources, and lead educational sessions on nutrition, food justice, and sustainable living. You can contribute by organizing workshops, sharing information at local events, or supporting digital campaigns to inspire action across communities.
